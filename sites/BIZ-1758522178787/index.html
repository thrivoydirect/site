About Radel

For years, Radel has been Chennai's trusted source for quality groceries and everyday essentials. We're more than just a store; we're your neighbors, committed to providing fresh, locally sourced produce whenever possible, alongside a wide selection of pantry staples, household items, and snacks. At Radel, you'll find competitive prices, a clean and welcoming environment, and friendly staff dedicated to making your shopping experience easy and enjoyable. We understand the importance of convenience, which is why we're conveniently located to serve the Chennai community. Whether you need ingredients for a traditional South Indian recipe or simply want to stock up on your favorites, Radel is here to help. Experience the difference of shopping with a local grocer who cares.
