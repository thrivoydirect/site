About Shospital

For over a decade, SHospital in Manipal has been dedicated to providing unparalleled assessment and treatment for the entire musculoskeletal system. From bones and joints to muscles, nerves, ligaments, and tendons, we address every aspect that impacts your movement and activity. Backed by the strength of Manipal Hospitals' 70 years of experience, 38 hospitals, 7200 doctors, and a network touching 60 million lives across 19 cities, we bring world-class expertise to your doorstep. Our core values are patient-first, with each doctor a human care expert committed to going above and beyond. At SHospital, we believe every life is priceless, and we're dedicated to helping you live life to the fullest. Join us and discover stories that reinforce your belief in 'Life's On'.
